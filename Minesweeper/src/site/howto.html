How To Play

What is Minesweeper?

Minesweeper is a game where the player starts off with a x by y grid and all tiles are hidden.
Under each tile is either a mine, blank, or a number that signifies the amount of mines that
surrounsd the tile. Your job is to logically deduce where all the mines are and flag them.

Controls

Left Click a tile to reveal it. Revealing a mine makes you lose.
Right Click a tile to flag/unflag it. Flagging all of the mines makes you win.
